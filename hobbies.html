Flower arranging

I discovered a passion for flower arranging earlier this year, and it has since become a captivating hobby. Working in a flower shop in preparation for Valentine's Day not only enhanced my skills but also deepened my love for creating beautiful floral arrangements. This hands-on experience enriched my understanding of the craft, making it a fulfilling and lasting endeavor.

Coding

My journey into coding began with the discovery of HTML. What started as a fascination has now evolved into a daily part of my routine. Coding has become an integral and enjoyable aspect of my day-to-day activities.

Korean Culture

My fascination with Korean culture began when my cousin asked for help with his Korean language exam. From that point, I delved into Korean series and films, developing an admiration for their unique storytelling. The journey extended to appreciating their cuisine and music, forming a lasting connection that continues to grow today.

Surfing

When I was in the Philippines, surfing was an annual tradition for me, a simple joy that meant a 5-hour trip to the waves. Since moving, the surfboard has taken a back seat. Dreaming of catching waves again if I ever make it back to the Philippines.
